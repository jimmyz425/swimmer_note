# Breaststroke Dry Land Training

> Part of [[swimming-strokes/breaststroke|Breaststroke Swimming Technique: Complete Training Reference]]

These exercises can be done outside the pool to develop strength, flexibility, and muscle memory for breaststroke techniques. Breaststroke benefits most from dry land training due to its unique kick mechanics.

---

## Ankle & Whip Kick Drills (Most Critical)

| Drill | Description | Focus Points |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Ankle Eversion Stretch** | Sit on floor, legs extended. Turn feet outward forcefully. Hold 10 seconds. 5 sets. | THE most critical drill for breaststroke. Feet MUST turn outward during kick. Develops the motion that makes legal whip kick. Feel the outside edge of foot engaging. |
| **Sit on Heels (Outward)** | Kneel, sit back on heels with feet turned outward. Hold 20-30 seconds. 3 sets. | Ankle flexibility in everted position. This is exactly the position feet need during whip kick propulsive phase. Builds flexibility over time. |
| **Ankle Dorsiflexion Stretch** | Stand facing wall, foot back, press heel down while flexing ankle. Hold 30 seconds each foot. | Ankle range for bringing heels to buttocks. Flexible ankles allow proper kick recovery. Tight ankles limit knee bend. |
| **Ankle Circles (Full Range)** | Sit or lie, rotate ankles in full circles. 20 circles each direction each foot. | General ankle mobility. Whip kick requires complex ankle motion. Full range of motion essential. |
| **Whip Kick Simulation (Standing)** | Stand on one leg, simulate whip kick motion with other leg: heel to bum, foot out, snap together. 10 reps each leg. | Practice the kick motion muscle memory. Feel: heels to bum (not knees), narrow knees, feet outward, snap together. Slow motion to feel each phase. |

## Hip & Adductor Drills

| Drill | Description | Focus Points |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Adductor Stretch (Butterfly)** | Sit, feet together, knees out. Lean forward gently. Hold 30 seconds. | Hip flexibility for narrow knees during kick. Flexible adductors allow knees to stay close together. Tight adductors force knees wide = drag. |
| **Hip Internal Rotation** | Sit with knees bent at 90 degrees, feet wider than knees. Let knees fall inward. Hold 30 seconds. | Hip mobility for whip kick mechanics. Internal rotation needed for bringing heels to buttocks. Develops range of motion. |
| **Glute Bridge with Adduction** | Lie on back, knees bent. Bridge up, squeeze knees together at top. 3 sets of 15. | Combines hip extension with knee squeeze. Mimics the snap phase of whip kick. Feel the final squeeze power. |

## Core & Body Position Drills

| Drill | Description | Focus Points |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Plank to Dolphin** | Start in plank, press chest toward floor, hips rise, return. 10 reps. | Mimics wave-style breaststroke body motion. Chest press down = hips rise. Feel the undulating motion used by elite swimmers. |
| **Dead Bug (Breaststroke)** | Lie on back, arms up, legs bent at 90 degrees. Pull arms down to sides as legs extend and snap together. 3 sets of 10. | Simulates the pull-kick coordination. Arms pull down (pull phase), legs extend and snap (kick phase). Feel the timing. |
| **Superman Hold** | Lie face-down, arms extended forward, lift arms and legs. Hold 15-30 seconds. 3 sets. | Streamline position strength. Long body position during glide. Core engaged to maintain alignment. |

## Arm Pull Drills

| Drill | Description | Focus Points |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Arm Pull Simulation** | Stand, arms forward. Turn hands out 45 degrees, sweep out, sweep in to chest, shoot forward. 20 reps. | Practice the pull motion muscle memory. Feel: hands turn out, narrow outsweep, elbows high during insweep, quick shoot forward. Do slowly to feel each phase. |
| **Resistance Band Pull (Breast)** | Stand with band in hands, arms forward. Pull: out-in-shoot pattern. 3 sets of 15. | Build pull strength through the pattern. Feel the acceleration during insweep. Keep elbows above hands. Quick recovery forward. |
| **Push-ups (Narrow)** | Standard push-ups with hands close together under shoulders. 3 sets of 10. | Develops insweep power. Narrow hand position mimics breaststroke pull. Elbows stay high during push-down. |

---

## Flexibility Drills

| Drill | Description | Focus Points |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Knee-to-Chest Stretch** | Stand, pull knee to chest. Hold 30 seconds each leg. | Hip flexibility for bringing heels to buttocks. Flexible hips allow proper knee bend. Tight hips force knees wide. |
| **Lower Back Stretch** | Lie on back, knees to chest, rock gently. 30 seconds. | Back flexibility for wave-style motion. Flexible spine allows undulation. Prevents back strain during breaststroke. |
| **Streamline Stretch** | Stand tall, arms extended overhead together. Hold 20 seconds. | Streamline position for glide phase. Arms tight together, body tall. Feel the glide position. |

---

## Timing & Coordination Drills

| Drill | Description | Focus Points |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Breaststroke Sequence Drill** | Stand, practice: Pull (arms) → Breathe (lift chin) → Kick (simulate) → Glide (pause). 20 reps. | Practice the timing sequence muscle memory. Say it aloud: "Pull, Breathe, Kick, Glide." Feel the pause during glide. No rushing. |
| **Jump Squat with Sequence** | Squat position. Jump up: Pull arms down, breathe, land: kick motion, hold: glide. 10 reps. | Full body coordination. Combines all breaststroke elements. Feel the rhythm: pull-breathe-kick-glide. |
