# Butterfly Dry Land Training

> Part of [[swimming-strokes/butterfly|Butterfly Swimming Technique Guide]]

These exercises can be done outside the pool to develop strength, flexibility, and muscle memory for butterfly techniques. Butterfly is the most strength-dependent stroke, making dry land training essential.

---

## Core & Undulation Drills (Foundation)

| Drill | Description | Focus Points |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Dolphin Plank** | Start in plank, press chest down toward floor, hips rise up, return. Continuous rhythm. 3 sets of 15. | THE essential butterfly drill. Mimics the whole-body undulation. Chest press initiates motion (same as in water). Feel the wave travel through body. Hips rise when chest drops. |
| **Cat-Cow Stretch** | On hands and knees, alternate between arching back up (cat) and dipping down (cow). 3 sets of 15. | Spine flexibility for undulation. Develops the wave motion through spine. Feel the chest press and hip lift sequence. |
| **Superman to Dolphin** | Lie face-down, arms extended. Lift arms and legs, then press chest down while lifting hips higher. 10 reps. | Combines extension with undulation. Feel the transition from extended position to wave motion. Core connects upper and lower body. |
| **Dead Bug Dolphin** | Lie on back, arms up, legs extended. Simulate dolphin motion: arms swing back as hips lift, reverse. 10 reps. | Core undulation from different angle. Feel the hip movement that powers dolphin kick. Core controls the wave motion. |

## Dolphin Kick Drills

| Drill | Description | Focus Points |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Standing Dolphin Kick** | Stand tall, simulate dolphin kick motion from hips. Small amplitude, hip-driven. 20 reps. | Practice hip-driven kick motion. NOT from knees - feel the hips initiating. Keep knees relatively straight. Power from glutes and core. |
| **Glute Bridge Pulse** | Lie on back, knees bent. Bridge up, then pulse hips up and down rapidly. 3 sets of 20 pulses. | Hip drive strength for dolphin kick. Glutes power the kick. Feel the rapid hip motion. Builds endurance for continuous kicks. |
| **Ankle Point/Flex Rapid** | Sit, point toes hard, then flex back rapidly. 3 sets of 30 rapid switches. | Ankle speed for dolphin kick propulsion. Quick ankle motion essential. Dolphin kick needs fast, flexible ankles. |

## Shoulder & Arm Drills

| Drill | Description | Focus Points |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Arm Stroke Simulation** | Stand bent forward at hips. Arms forward: enter, catch (high elbow), pull through, recover low. 20 reps. | Practice full arm cycle muscle memory. Feel: fingertips enter, high elbow catch, keyhole pull pattern, low relaxed recovery. Do slowly to feel each phase. |
| **Resistance Band Butterfly Pull** | Stand, band in hands overhead. Pull down through keyhole pattern, accelerate past hips. 3 sets of 15. | Build pull strength. Feel the acceleration through pull. High elbows throughout. Power from back muscles (lats), not just arms. |
| **Medicine Ball Slam** | Stand, lift med ball overhead, slam down forcefully. 3 sets of 10. | Power for pull phase. Accelerate through motion (same as butterfly pull). Full body engagement. Feel the power transfer from core to arms. |
| **Shoulder Blade Squeeze** | Arms at sides, squeeze shoulder blades together, hold 5 seconds. 3 sets of 10. | Scapular stability for arm mechanics. Stable shoulders during pull and recovery. Prevents shoulder injury from butterfly stress. |

## Recovery Drills

| Drill | Description | Focus Points |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Low Recovery Drill (Standing)** | Stand, simulate butterfly recovery with arms low and relaxed, slightly bent. 20 reps. | Practice low recovery muscle memory. Arms stay LOW over water level (imaginary). Relaxed, not rigid. Bent slightly, not straight. Feel the efficiency. |
| **Arm Swing Stretch** | Large arm swings forward and backward, relaxed. 10 each direction. | Shoulder mobility for recovery. Relaxed arms during recovery. Prevents tension that causes fatigue. |
| **Lat Stretch (Wall)** | Arm up against wall, lean away. Hold 30 seconds each side. | Lat flexibility for full arm range. Lats are primary butterfly muscles. Flexible lats allow full reach and powerful pull. |

---

## Timing & Rhythm Drills

| Drill | Description | Focus Points |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Two-Kick Sequence Drill** | Stand, simulate: First kick (small) with arm entry, Second kick (large) with arm push. Say "Enter-kick, Push-kick." 20 reps. | Practice two-kick timing muscle memory. Feel the smaller first kick, larger second kick. NEVER single kick. The rhythm becomes automatic. |
| **Full Stroke Simulation** | Stand bent forward. Complete sequence: Enter-kick, Pull-kick-push, Recover. Continuous motion. 10 reps continuous. | Whole stroke timing. No pauses between phases. Feel the continuous rhythm. Arms and body connected as one unit. |
| **Metronome Drill** | Stand, perform arm stroke simulation to metronome beat (60-80 BPM). Match motion to rhythm. | Rhythm development. Consistent tempo. Continuous motion matching the beat. Develops the timing sense for butterfly. |

## Strength & Endurance Drills

| Drill | Description | Focus Points |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Push-ups** | Standard push-ups. 3 sets of 10-15. | General upper body strength. Butterfly is strength-dependent. Strong chest and arms power the pull. |
| **Pull-ups** | Standard pull-ups or assisted. 3 sets of 5-10. | Back strength for pull phase. Lats are primary butterfly muscles. Strong back = powerful pull. |
| **Burpees** | Full burpee with jump. 3 sets of 10. | Full body power and endurance. Butterfly requires sustained power. Builds the conditioning for continuous effort. |
| **Mountain Climbers** | Plank position, drive knees forward rapidly. 3 sets of 30 seconds. | Core endurance for dolphin motion. Rapid hip motion similar to dolphin kick. Core stability throughout. |

---

## Flexibility Drills

| Drill | Description | Focus Points |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Hip Flexor Deep Stretch** | Deep kneeling lunge, push hips forward. Hold 30 seconds each side. | Hip flexibility for dolphin kick range. Dolphin motion needs flexible hips. Tight hips limit undulation. |
| **Ankle Mobility All Directions** | Sit, move ankles through all ranges: point, flex, turn in, turn out, circles. 2 minutes each foot. | Comprehensive ankle mobility. Dolphin kick needs ankles that move freely in all directions. Build complete flexibility. |
| **Streamline Stretch** | Stand tall, arms overhead together. Hold 30 seconds. | Streamline position for entry and glide. Arms tight, body tall. The position for every entry. |
